Eligibility
  • Job applicants must meet the minimum age requirement of 18 years old.
  • Applicant must provide accurate and truthful information in their job
    applications.
Job Application Process
  • All job applications must be submitted through the online portal on the
    Musca Fashion Chic website.
  • Applicants must complete all required fields and provide relevant documents
    or attachments as requested
Communication
  • Musca Fashion Chic will communicate with applicants primarily through the contact
    details provided in their job application. It is the responsibility of the applicant
    to ensure the accuracy and availability of their contact information.
